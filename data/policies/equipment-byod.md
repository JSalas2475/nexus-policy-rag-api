# Equipment and BYOD Policy

## Company Equipment

Nexus Technologies provides necessary equipment to all full-time employees. Standard equipment packages vary by role.

## Standard Equipment Package

| Role | Equipment |
|------|-----------|
| Engineering | MacBook Pro 14", 27" monitor, keyboard, mouse, headset |
| Sales | MacBook Air 13", headset, mobile phone stipend |
| Operations | Windows laptop, dual monitors, keyboard, mouse |
| All roles | Access to cloud-based collaboration tools |

Equipment is ordered within **5 business days** of hire and shipped to the employee's home or office address.

## Equipment Lifecycle

Laptops are refreshed every **3 years** or sooner if damaged beyond repair. Employees may request early refresh with IT approval if performance issues affect productivity. Old equipment must be returned to IT for secure data wiping and recycling.

## Bring Your Own Device (BYOD)

BYOD is permitted only for mobile phones used with company MDM (Mobile Device Management) enrollment. Personal laptops and tablets may **not** be used for accessing company data or systems.

### BYOD Requirements
- Device must be enrolled in company MDM within 24 hours
- Passcode or biometric lock required
- Remote wipe capability must be enabled
- Company may remotely wipe corporate data upon termination

## Equipment Care

Employees are responsible for reasonable care of company equipment. Damage due to negligence may result in partial replacement cost charged to the employee (up to **$250**). Report damage or loss to IT within 24 hours.

## Lost or Stolen Equipment

Report lost or stolen equipment immediately to IT and security@nexustech.com. For stolen equipment, file a police report within 48 hours. IT will remotely disable the device within 2 hours of report.

## Return of Equipment

All company equipment must be returned within **5 business days** of termination or role change. IT will provide a prepaid shipping label for remote employees. Failure to return equipment may result in deduction from final paycheck as permitted by law.

## Software Installation

Only IT-approved software may be installed on company devices. Employees may request additional software through the IT Service Portal. Unauthorized software installation is a security violation.

## Home Office Equipment

Full remote employees receive a **$500 one-time stipend** for home office setup (see Remote Work Policy). Stipend-eligible items include desk, chair, monitor stand, and ergonomic accessories. Receipts required.
