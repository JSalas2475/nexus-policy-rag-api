# Expense Reimbursement Policy

## Scope

This policy governs reimbursement of business-related expenses incurred by Nexus Technologies employees. All expenses must be reasonable, necessary, and directly related to company business.

## General Guidelines

Employees must obtain pre-approval for any single expense exceeding **$500**. All expenses must be submitted within **30 days** of incurring the expense via the Concur expense system. Receipts are required for all expenses over $25.

## Travel Expenses

### Air Travel
Employees must book economy class for flights under 6 hours. Business class requires VP approval for flights over 6 hours. Flights must be booked at least 14 days in advance when possible.

### Lodging
The nightly lodging cap is **$200** in major metropolitan areas and **$150** elsewhere. Employees should use preferred hotel partners listed in the travel portal.

### Meals During Travel
Daily meal allowance while traveling is **$75** per day. Alcohol is not reimbursable unless entertaining approved clients with prior manager approval.

## Ground Transportation

Rideshare services (Uber, Lyft) are reimbursable for business travel. Personal vehicle mileage is reimbursed at the IRS standard rate (**$0.67 per mile** for 2025). Commuting to the regular office is not reimbursable.

## Client Entertainment

Client entertainment expenses up to **$150 per person** require manager pre-approval. Expenses exceeding $150 per person require director approval. All client entertainment must include a business purpose description.

## Non-Reimbursable Expenses

The following are not reimbursable:
- Personal grooming or clothing (unless required safety gear)
- Traffic violations and parking tickets
- Spouse or family travel expenses
- Gym memberships and personal fitness
- Home internet for hybrid employees (stipend provided separately)

## Corporate Credit Cards

Employees issued corporate credit cards must reconcile transactions within 5 business days of statement close. Misuse of corporate cards may result in disciplinary action up to termination.

## Processing Timeline

Approved reimbursements are processed within **10 business days** and deposited via direct deposit. Expenses submitted after the 30-day deadline require director approval and may be denied.

## Audit

Finance conducts random audits of expense reports quarterly. Fraudulent expense submissions will result in immediate termination and potential legal action.
