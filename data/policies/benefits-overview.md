# Employee Benefits Overview

## Overview

Nexus Technologies offers a comprehensive benefits package to eligible full-time employees. Eligibility begins on the **first day of the month following 30 days of employment**.

## Health Insurance

Nexus Technologies offers medical, dental, and vision insurance through BlueCross Premier. The company pays **80% of employee premium** costs and **60% of dependent premium** costs.

### Medical Plan Options
- **PPO Plan**: $500 individual / $1,000 family deductible; $30 copay for primary care
- **HDHP Plan**: $1,500 individual / $3,000 family deductible; HSA eligible with $750 company contribution

Open enrollment occurs each November for coverage effective January 1.

## Retirement Benefits

Nexus Technologies offers a 401(k) plan with **4% company match** on employee contributions up to 6% of salary. Employees are eligible for the match after **90 days** of employment. Vesting schedule: 25% per year, fully vested after 4 years.

## Life and Disability Insurance

Company-provided life insurance covers **1x annual salary** up to $500,000. Short-term disability covers 60% of salary for up to 12 weeks. Long-term disability covers 60% of salary after 90 days of disability.

## Wellness Programs

The company wellness program includes:
- $50 monthly gym reimbursement
- Annual health screening (on-site, free)
- Mental health support via ModernHealth (8 free sessions per year)
- Employee Assistance Program (EAP) available 24/7

## PTO Summary

Refer to the PTO Policy for full details. Summary:
- New employees: 15 PTO days + 5 sick days annually
- 10 company-paid holidays per year
- Parental leave: 12 weeks paid (primary caregiver), 4 weeks paid (secondary caregiver)

## Education Assistance

Tuition reimbursement up to **$5,250 per calendar year** for job-related courses and degree programs. Employees must maintain a grade of B or better and remain employed for 12 months after course completion.

## Commuter Benefits

Pre-tax commuter benefits up to $300/month for public transit and parking. Available to employees working at least 3 days per week in the office.
