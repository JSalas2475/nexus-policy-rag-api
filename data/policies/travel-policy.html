Business Travel Policy

Nexus Technologies employees who travel for business must follow these guidelines.

Pre-Trip Approval

All business travel requires manager approval via the Travel Request Form at least 7 days before departure. International travel requires director approval. Travel to high-risk countries requires CEO and Security approval.

Booking

All travel must be booked through the company travel portal (Concur Travel). Personal bookings are not eligible for reimbursement. Employees should select the lowest reasonable fare available.

Per Diem Rates

  • Domestic travel: $75/day meals, $200/night lodging (major cities)
  • International travel: Per GSA international per diem rates
  • Incidentals: $15/day included in meal allowance

Travel Insurance

Company-provided travel insurance covers medical emergencies, trip cancellation, and lost baggage up to $3,000. Employees should enroll via the benefits portal before international trips.

Travel During COVID or Health Advisories

Travel to areas under CDC Level 3 or higher advisories requires VP approval. Employees who become ill during travel must notify their manager and HR immediately.

Non-Compliance

Expenses for unapproved travel will not be reimbursed. Repeated non-compliance may result in loss of travel privileges.
