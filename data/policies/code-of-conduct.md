# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

Nexus Technologies is dedicated to maintaining a workplace where all employees are treated with dignity and respect. This Code of Conduct applies to all employees, contractors, and visitors.

## Professional Behavior

Employees must conduct themselves professionally at all times. Expected behaviors include:
- Treating colleagues, customers, and partners with respect
- Communicating openly and honestly
- Collaborating effectively across teams
- Taking responsibility for one's actions and decisions

## Anti-Harassment Policy

Nexus Technologies has zero tolerance for harassment of any kind. Harassment includes unwelcome conduct based on race, color, religion, sex, national origin, age, disability, sexual orientation, gender identity, or any other protected characteristic.

Examples of prohibited conduct include:
- Offensive jokes, slurs, or epithets
- Unwelcome physical contact
- Displaying offensive images or materials
- Retaliation against anyone who reports harassment

## Reporting Procedures

Employees who experience or witness harassment should report it immediately to their manager, HR, or via the anonymous Ethics Hotline at **1-888-NEXUS-ETHICS**. All reports will be investigated within **5 business days**. Retaliation against reporters is strictly prohibited and will result in termination.

## Conflicts of Interest

Employees must disclose any personal, financial, or external business interests that could conflict with company duties. Disclosure must be made to HR within **10 days** of becoming aware of the conflict. Outside employment requires written manager approval.

## Gifts and Entertainment

Employees may accept gifts valued at **$50 or less** from business partners. Gifts exceeding $50 must be declined or approved by a director. Cash gifts are never acceptable regardless of amount.

## Confidentiality

Employees must protect confidential company information during and after employment. Confidential information includes business strategies, customer data, financial information, and unreleased product details.

## Social Media

Employees who identify themselves as Nexus Technologies employees on social media must include a disclaimer that views are their own. Employees may not disclose confidential information or speak on behalf of the company without authorization.

## Disciplinary Actions

Violations of this Code may result in disciplinary action including verbal warning, written warning, suspension, or termination depending on severity. Criminal conduct will be reported to law enforcement.

## Acknowledgment

All employees must sign an acknowledgment of this Code of Conduct during onboarding and annually thereafter.
