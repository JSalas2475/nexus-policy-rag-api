# Paid Time Off (PTO) Policy

## Overview

Nexus Technologies provides paid time off (PTO) to eligible full-time employees for rest, personal matters, and vacation. This policy applies to all regular full-time employees in the United States.

## PTO Accrual for New Employees

New full-time employees receive **15 PTO days annually**, accrued on a bi-weekly basis starting from their hire date. During the first year of employment, employees may use PTO as it accrues. Part-time employees (20–29 hours per week) accrue PTO on a prorated basis at 10 days annually.

## PTO Accrual by Tenure

| Tenure | Annual PTO Days |
|--------|-----------------|
| 0–2 years | 15 days |
| 3–5 years | 20 days |
| 6+ years | 25 days |

Tenure-based increases take effect on the employee's anniversary date.

## Requesting PTO

Employees must submit PTO requests through the HR portal at least **5 business days** in advance for requests of 3 or more consecutive days. Managers must approve or deny requests within 2 business days. During peak business periods (November 15–January 15), PTO may be limited to no more than 10 consecutive days without VP approval.

## PTO Carryover

Employees may carry over up to **5 unused PTO days** into the next calendar year. Any PTO balance exceeding 5 days on December 31 will be forfeited unless state law requires otherwise. Carried-over PTO must be used by March 31 of the following year.

## Sick Leave

In addition to PTO, employees receive **5 sick days** per year for personal illness or medical appointments. Sick days do not carry over and are separate from PTO. Employees may not use sick days for vacation purposes.

## PTO Payout on Termination

Upon voluntary resignation, unused PTO up to the annual accrual cap will be paid out in the final paycheck. Employees terminated for cause forfeit unused PTO unless required by state law.

## Blackout Periods

Engineering teams may have department-specific blackout periods during major product releases. Blackout periods must be communicated at least 30 days in advance and cannot exceed 14 consecutive days.

## Manager Responsibilities

Managers must ensure adequate team coverage when approving PTO. Teams should maintain at least 75% staffing during any given week. Managers who fail to respond to PTO requests within 2 business days will receive an automated escalation to their director.
