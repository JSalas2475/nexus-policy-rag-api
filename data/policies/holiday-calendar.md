# Company Holiday Calendar 2025

## Paid Holidays

Nexus Technologies observes the following **10 paid company holidays** in 2025. Full-time employees receive holiday pay; part-time employees receive prorated holiday pay based on their schedule.

## Floating Holidays

In addition to the 10 fixed holidays, employees receive **2 floating holidays** per year. Floating holidays must be used within the calendar year and require manager approval. Floating holidays cannot be used during the last two weeks of December without director approval.

## Holiday Pay

Non-exempt employees who work on a company holiday receive **1.5x their regular hourly rate** plus an alternate day off within 30 days. Exempt employees who work on holidays do not receive additional pay but may take an alternate day off with manager approval.

## Office Closures

All Nexus Technologies offices are closed on paid holidays. Essential personnel in Operations and Security may be scheduled with compensatory time off provided within 30 days.

## Regional Variations

Employees in international offices follow the holiday calendar for their country. US holidays listed above apply to all US-based employees regardless of remote status.

## PTO Interaction

Paid holidays do not count against PTO balances. If a holiday falls during an employee's approved PTO period, the day is treated as a paid holiday, not a PTO day.
