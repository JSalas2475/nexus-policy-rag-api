NEXUS TECHNOLOGIES - ONBOARDING AND PROBATION POLICY

SECTION 1: ONBOARDING OVERVIEW
New employees at Nexus Technologies complete a structured 30-60-90 day onboarding program designed to integrate them into the company culture, systems, and role expectations.

SECTION 2: PRE-START REQUIREMENTS
Before Day 1, new hires must complete:
- Background check (conditional offer contingent on clear results)
- I-9 employment verification within 3 business days of start
- Signed offer letter, NDA, and Code of Conduct acknowledgment
- Direct deposit and tax form (W-4) setup in HR portal

SECTION 3: FIRST WEEK
Day 1 activities include:
- Welcome orientation session (2 hours, HR-led)
- IT equipment setup and account provisioning
- Benefits enrollment overview
- Manager 1:1 to review role expectations and 30-day goals
- Buddy assignment for cultural integration

All new hires must complete mandatory compliance training within the first 5 business days:
- Information Security Awareness
- Code of Conduct
- Anti-Harassment
- Data Privacy Fundamentals

SECTION 4: PROBATIONARY PERIOD
All new full-time employees serve a 90-day probationary period. During probation:
- Either party may terminate employment with 1 week notice
- Benefits eligibility begins after 30 days (see Benefits Overview)
- Remote work requests require director approval during probation
- Performance check-in at 30, 60, and 90 days

SECTION 5: PROBATION COMPLETION
At 90 days, the manager submits a Probation Review in Workday. Outcomes:
- Pass: Employee confirmed as regular full-time employee
- Extend: Probation extended up to 30 additional days with documented plan
- Fail: Employment terminated

SECTION 6: ONBOARDING RESPONSIBILITIES
- HR: Orientation, paperwork, benefits enrollment support
- IT: Equipment, accounts, access provisioning within 24 hours of start
- Manager: Role training, goal setting, weekly 1:1s during first month
- Buddy: Informal support, office navigation, culture introduction

SECTION 7: 30-60-90 DAY GOALS
New employees work with their manager to establish:
- 30-day: Learn systems, meet team, complete training
- 60-day: Begin independent contributions, own small projects
- 90-day: Fully ramped, probation review, first performance goals set
