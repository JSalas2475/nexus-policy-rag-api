# Information Security Policy

## Purpose

Nexus Technologies is committed to protecting company, customer, and employee data. This policy establishes minimum security requirements for all personnel and systems.

## Password Requirements

All employees must use passwords that meet the following criteria:
- Minimum **14 characters**
- At least one uppercase letter, one lowercase letter, one number, and one special character
- Passwords must be changed every **90 days**
- Passwords may not reuse any of the last **12 passwords**

Passwords must not be shared, written down, or stored in plain text. Use of a company-approved password manager is required.

## Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)

MFA is **mandatory** for all employees accessing company systems, including email, VPN, cloud services, and source code repositories. Approved MFA methods include hardware security keys (preferred), authenticator apps (Google Authenticator, Authy), and push notifications via Okta Verify.

MFA must be enabled within **3 business days** of hire. Employees who fail to enable MFA will have system access suspended until compliance is achieved.

## Access Control

Access to systems follows the principle of least privilege. Access requests must be approved by the employee's manager and the system owner. Access reviews are conducted quarterly. Terminated employees have all access revoked within **2 hours** of separation.

## Data Classification

| Level | Description | Examples |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| Public | Approved for external release | Marketing materials |
| Internal | Internal use only | Org charts, policies |
| Confidential | Restricted access | Customer data, financials |
| Restricted | Highest sensitivity | SSNs, payment card data |

Confidential and Restricted data must be encrypted at rest and in transit.

## Device Security

Company laptops must have full-disk encryption enabled, automatic OS updates, and endpoint detection and response (EDR) software installed. USB storage devices are prohibited unless explicitly authorized by IT. Screen locks must activate after **5 minutes** of inactivity.

## Phishing and Social Engineering

Employees must report suspected phishing emails to security@nexustech.com within 1 hour. Mandatory security awareness training is required annually. Employees who click simulated phishing links must complete remedial training within 5 business days.

## Incident Reporting

Security incidents must be reported to the Security Operations Center (SOC) within **1 hour** of discovery via the incident hotline (1-800-NEXUS-SOC) or security@nexustech.com. Failure to report incidents promptly may result in disciplinary action.

## Acceptable Use

Company systems may not be used for illegal activities, cryptocurrency mining, unauthorized software installation, or accessing inappropriate content. Personal use of company systems is limited and must not interfere with work duties.

## Violations

Security policy violations may result in disciplinary action up to and including termination and legal prosecution.
