# Remote Work and Hybrid Policy

## Purpose

Nexus Technologies supports flexible work arrangements that balance employee autonomy with business needs. This policy defines eligibility, expectations, and procedures for remote and hybrid work.

## Eligibility

All full-time employees who have completed their **90-day probationary period** are eligible to request remote or hybrid work arrangements. New hires may work remotely only with written director approval during probation.

## Hybrid Schedule

The standard hybrid schedule requires employees to work **3 days per week in the office** (Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday) and up to 2 days remotely (Monday and Friday). Departments may adjust core in-office days with VP approval.

## Full Remote Work

Full remote work is available to employees in approved roles. Eligible roles include Software Engineer, Data Analyst, Technical Writer, and Customer Support Specialist. Full remote employees must reside in a state where Nexus Technologies is registered to do business.

## Home Office Requirements

Remote employees must maintain a dedicated workspace with reliable internet (minimum **50 Mbps download**). Nexus Technologies provides a one-time **$500 home office stipend** for eligible full remote employees hired after January 1, 2024.

## Equipment

Company-issued laptops must be used for all work activities. Remote employees receive standard equipment: laptop, monitor, keyboard, and mouse. Equipment remains company property and must be returned upon termination.

## Communication Expectations

Remote employees must be available during core hours of **10:00 AM – 4:00 PM** in their local time zone. Employees must respond to Slack messages within 1 hour during core hours and attend all mandatory meetings via video.

## Security Requirements

Remote employees must connect through the company VPN when accessing internal systems. Personal devices may not be used to access customer data. Screen locks must activate after 5 minutes of inactivity.

## Request Process

Employees submit a Flexible Work Request Form to their manager and HR. Requests are reviewed within 10 business days. Arrangements are renewed annually and may be modified or revoked based on performance or business needs.

## International Remote Work

International remote work is not permitted without explicit CEO approval and legal review. Temporary work from abroad is limited to **14 days per calendar year** for eligible employees.

## Performance Standards

Remote and hybrid employees are held to the same performance standards as in-office employees. Failure to meet availability or security requirements may result in revocation of remote work privileges.
