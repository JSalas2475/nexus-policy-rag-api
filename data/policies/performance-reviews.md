# Performance Review Policy

## Purpose

Nexus Technologies conducts regular performance reviews to provide feedback, recognize achievements, identify development opportunities, and align individual contributions with company goals.

## Review Cycle

Performance reviews occur **twice per year**:
- **Mid-Year Review**: July 1–31 (progress check, no compensation changes)
- **Annual Review**: January 15–February 15 (comprehensive review with compensation decisions)

New employees receive their first review after completing **6 months** of employment.

## Review Process

1. **Self-Assessment**: Employee completes self-assessment in Workday by assigned deadline
2. **Manager Review**: Manager evaluates performance against goals and competencies
3. **Calibration**: Leadership teams calibrate ratings across departments
4. **Feedback Meeting**: Manager delivers review in a 1:1 meeting within 5 business days of calibration
5. **Acknowledgment**: Employee acknowledges review in Workday

## Performance Rating Scale

| Rating | Description | Distribution Target |
|--------|-------------|---------------------|
| 5 - Exceptional | Consistently exceeds all expectations | 10% |
| 4 - Exceeds | Frequently exceeds expectations | 20% |
| 3 - Meets | Consistently meets expectations | 50% |
| 2 - Developing | Partially meets expectations | 15% |
| 1 - Unsatisfactory | Does not meet expectations | 5% |

## Goal Setting

Employees set **3–5 SMART goals** annually in collaboration with their manager. Goals must align with department and company objectives. Goals are reviewed and updated quarterly.

## Compensation Link

Annual review ratings directly impact:
- Merit salary increases (0–8% based on rating and budget)
- Annual bonus eligibility (ratings 1–2 are not eligible)
- Promotion consideration

Merit increases are effective **April 1** following the annual review cycle.

## Performance Improvement Plans (PIP)

Employees rated 1 or low 2 may be placed on a Performance Improvement Plan. PIPs last **60 days** and include specific measurable objectives. Successful completion returns employee to standard review cycle; unsuccessful completion may result in termination.

## 360-Degree Feedback

Optional 360-degree feedback is available for manager-level and above during annual reviews. Participants include peers, direct reports, and cross-functional partners.

## Disputes

Employees who disagree with their review may request a review discussion with HR within **10 business days** of acknowledgment. HR will facilitate a conversation but does not change manager ratings.

## Documentation

All reviews are documented in Workday and retained for **7 years**. Managers must not maintain separate unofficial review notes.
